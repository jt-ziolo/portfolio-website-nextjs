This Cookie Policy explains how Telomeric Software LLC (DBA JT Ziolo) ("we", "us", or "our") uses cookies and similar technologies on our portfolio website (the "Site"). It explains what these technologies are and why we use them, as well as your rights to control our use of them.

### What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small data files that are placed on your computer or mobile device when you visit a website. They are widely used to make websites work more efficiently, as well as to provide reporting information.

### How We Use Cookies

We use cookies for the following purposes:

1. **Essential cookies**: These cookies are necessary for the Site to function properly. They enable core functionality such as security, network management, and accessibility. You may not opt-out of these cookies.

2. **Analytics cookies**: We use Google Analytics to help us understand how visitors interact with our Site. These cookies collect information about your use of our Site, including which pages you go to most often and if you get error messages from certain pages. These cookies don't collect information that individually identifies you.

3. **Form data preservation**: We use cookies to temporarily store information you enter into our email contact form. This helps prevent data loss if you accidentally navigate away from the page or if your browser crashes before submitting the form.

4. **Next.js functionality**: Our Site uses Next.js, which may require certain cookies for optimal performance and functionality. These cookies help with things like routing, server-side rendering, and maintaining user sessions.

### Your Choices Regarding Cookies

You can set your browser to refuse all or some browser cookies, or to alert you when websites set or access cookies. If you disable or refuse cookies, please note that some parts of this Site may become inaccessible or not function properly.

### Google Analytics Opt-out

You can opt out of Google Analytics tracking by using the Google Analytics Opt-Out Browser Add-on, available at [https://tools.google.com/dlpage/gaoptout](https://tools.google.com/dlpage/gaoptout).
